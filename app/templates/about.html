About Us

We are a team of four students at the University of Central Florida. The webapp we created is part of our final project in the Data Analysis and Visualization course. The goal of this project is to create a useful tool by utilizing the skills we've learned over the past six months, including exploratory data analysis, python, html, css, javascript, databases, and machine learning.


This tools purpose is to give the user useful statics based on public COVID-19 data sets to help them understand risks of the virus and the importance of wearing a mask. We also included a fun widget that takes a photo of the user and analyses their face to see if they are wearing a mask. Based on the outcome, useful facts are displayed on the screen.
